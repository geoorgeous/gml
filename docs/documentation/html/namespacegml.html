Multiplies two quaternion rotations together.

Multiplying two quaternion rotations together this way results in a Quaternion that is the combination of the two Quaternion operand's roations.

Parameters
lhsA Quaternion that will be multiplied with the right Quaternion operand.
rhsA Quaternion that will be multiplied with the left Quaternion operand.
Returns
A quaternion which is the product of the left and right Quaternion operands.
Warning
Quaternion multiplication is not commutitive.
